Proper Running Shoes

During Phys. Ed., you want to wear clothing that is loose and comfortable.

It is recommended to also wear comfortable running shoes, and try to refrain from wearing jewellery to Physical Education.

In order to be able to partucupate in activites during Phys. Ed. class,

you MUST be wearing proper running shoes or any closed shoe with an arc and ankle support.
